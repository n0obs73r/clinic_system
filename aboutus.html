About Us




About Our Bangalore Hip and Knee Clinic

• With the motto of “Mobility is Life”, Bangalore Hip & Knee Clinic aims at providing speciality Orthopaedic care for people with hip and knee ailments with restricted mobility compromizing their normal lifestyle.

• Mainly focusing on People requiring Hip & Knee Joint Preservation and Joint Replacement surgeries along with Hip & Knee Arthroscopy Procedures, Complex Trauma, Limb Reconstruction and Deformity Correction Surgeries of the Lower Limbs.

• With additional attention to upper limb ailments requiring joint replacement & arthroscopy (Shouler & Elbow), deformity corrections and trauma care.

• We provide comprehensive outpatient orthopaedic speciality care including – diagnostic imaging (high quality digital x ray, CT Scan and ultrasound scans), state of the art physiotherapy services (Physiotatva.. Link) and laboratory facility.
