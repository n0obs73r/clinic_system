Dr Pradeep A Ramesh

M.S Orthopaedics, FJRS, FASM

Consultant Joint Replacement & Reconstruction Surgeon

Sparsh Hospital, Infantry Road, Bangalore.

Dr Pradeep A Ramesh is a Consultant Joint Replacement & Reconstruction Surgeon working at Sparsh Hospital, Bangalore. He specializes in hip and knee joint replacement surgery; complex trauma, limb reconstruction & deformity correction surgeries and arthroscopy surgery.
After completing his graduation (2002-2008), he pursued M.S Orthopaedics at KIMS, Bangalore (2009-2012). He later underwent higher surgical training at the internationally reputed Ganga Hospital, Coimbatore for the next 5 years during which he worked as a senior resident in the department of Trauma & Limb Reconstruction Surgeries (2012-2014), Fellow in Arthroscopy & Sports Medicine (2014-2015) and later as a Fellow in Joint Replacement Surgeries (2015-2017) gaining vast surgical experience and knowledge being with some of the world renowed Orthopaedic surgeons before joining Sparsh hospital at Bangalore in 2018.
Dr Pradeep A Ramesh has given many scientific presentations at State & National level conferences. He has authored and published noticeable research papers in National and International Journals and continues to involve actively in many ongoing research and academic activities.
To his credict, he has the Ranawat Orthopaedic Conference (ROC) – 2018 – Best Research Paper Award and the Indian Society of Hip & Knee Surgeons (ISHKS) – 2019 – Best Research Paper Award.
